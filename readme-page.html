üéÆ GAME CONTROLS

Master these essential controls to navigate through BIPARDY's strategic gameplay:

  • HELP: Reveals the next letter in sequence. Cost varies by word length (see Help System Economics above). Guarantees progress but reduces your score.
  • SKIP: Bypass the current word entirely. Preserves your valuable streak, awards zero points, and counts as a completed word toward your target goal.
  • SUBMIT: Submit your completed word guess for validation. Only becomes active when you've filled in all the missing letters.
  • Game Length Toggle: Choose between 12 or 24 word games (standard BIP-39 mnemonic lengths). Set before starting your game.
  • Word Length Toggle: Reveal or hide the total word length. Shows you how many letters the complete word contains, but permanently removes the 42% bonus for that specific word.

üìä Player Statistics:

  • Streak: Your current consecutive correct guesses. Resets to 0 on any wrong answer, but skips preserve your streak. Critical for multiplayer combined scores.
  • Words: Total count of words you've dealt with - includes successful guesses, help-completed words, and skipped words. Excludes words where you failed with 2 wrong guesses.
